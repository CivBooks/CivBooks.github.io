A 5.0 Manifesto§0 §0-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-=-A summary of concepts for consideration in future iterations of the CivClassics server.
Page 2 of 12
Reinforcement§0 §0-------------------* Remove reinforcement groups, have all reinforcements be equal to all players regardless of who reinforces.§0 §0* Match maturation times with current acid times.
Page 3 of 12
* Chests become locked by assigning them to renamed papers, opening a locked chest requires having the paper in inventory.§0 §0* Replace acid with "solvent", expensive item that takes up resources for effectively doing the /ctb command.
Page 4 of 12
* Solvents would be roughly in resource value to their corrosponding reinforcement material, requires mass resources in factories to make.§0 §0* Destroys reinforcement material in use.
Page 5 of 12
Farming§0 §0-------------------* Double all crop times.
Page 6 of 12
Bastions§0 §0-------------------* Remove them.
Page 7 of 12
Map Generation§0 §0-------------------* Remove a large chunk of nether islands, situate them further to the map fringes.§0 §0* Larger, uninterrupted land masses, larger biomes, less rivers in favour of fewer, longer ones.§0
Page 8 of 12
* Divide map between a small few continents, spread between oceans, each reasonably consistent in biomes, less or more of each kind.§0 §0* More seas, gulfs, basins.§0 §0* Mountain ranges dividing areas of a continent.
Page 9 of 12
* Square pyramid-like structures of bedrock underneath large mountains such as in ranges.§0 §0* Surplus and deficit of iron and diamonds veins in certain regions, heavier distribution near mountain ranges.
Page 10 of 12
* Allow certain lakes and basins to go below or above sea level.§0 §0* 20km radius.§0 §0* Man-made structures (a la dungeons but bigger) in world on spawn with loot, reinforced with stone, very rare.
