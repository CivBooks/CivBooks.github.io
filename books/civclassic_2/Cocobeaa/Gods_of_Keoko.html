a bright male energy that penetrated the feminine darkness, creating a universe of polarities; black and white, male and female, etc. On earth, the light of the sun penetrated the darkness of the soil to bring forth all living things.§0

At this time the Great Spirits were born in Keoko. Kanhe was the first; as the eldest Spirit he ruled over the others, and is the spirit of creation, sunlight, fresh water, and forests. He was the male ancestor of all forms of life, including people.

Next came Tangaroa ( Kanaloa ) of the Ocean, followed by Tu, who is the patron of all of the works and skills of men, and Rongo, the Spirit of agriculture and the healing arts.
