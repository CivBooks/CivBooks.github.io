=================== CHAPTER ONE§0 §0 Who Are We?§0 §0===================The Aznazian Federalist People's Party is the name of a movement originating in the Aznazian capitol of Vacari. We are a group of people from all walks of life, united to pursue

common goals. We welcome any members, only requiring citizenship in the Nation of Aznazia. Our dues are low (1 AZM per Month) and the reward is high. The AFPP is a place to form lasting and beneficial relationships for all people.

=================== CHAPTER TWO§0 §0 What Do We Believe?§0 §0===================Our party has a slogan. "Peace, Security, and Prosperity. Forever." This isn't just a tagline for us, it is a promise. In the following section I will show you what we mean.
Page 5 of 12
1) Peace§0 §0Peace is a paramount goal to the AFPP. Both peace in the domestic sense and in the foreign. We are against war, and against foreign intervention. We support cooperation between Nations and are League Friendly.
Page 6 of 12
2) Security§0 §0Security doesn't just mean keeping danger out, it also means keeping safety in. The AFPP stands for cooperative citizens Unions to ensure the fair treatment of all people. We also support the right to National Self-Determination for all people
Page 7 of 12
3) Prosperity§0 §0We support the 3 Points Policy for economic prosperity in our Nation. We believe there is great possibility for strength and growth in our economy, requiring only the most minimal of regulation.

=================== CHAPTER THREE§0 §0 The 3 Points Policy§0 §0===================The 3PP is a comprehensive plan for the economic prosperity of Aznazia. Its 3 points are International Trade, The Aznazian Mark, and AzWorks.
Page 9 of 12
International Trade§0 §0~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~As part of our belief in Peace we promote free and open trade between Nations. Our Gov't will only interfere in these for Security and Currency Exchange. This will help to build a free and peaceful world.
Page 10 of 12
The Aznazian Mark§0 §0~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~The Party supports the issuing of a national currency backed by both Gold and Iron. This Aznazian Mark (AZM) will help to standardise exchange and promote commerce. This currency will have a stable value and not inflate.
Page 11 of 12
Public Works Projects§0 §0~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~The AFPP Public Works Project, known as AzWorks, will revolutionize the roads, walls, rails, and farms of the nation of Aznazia. This will jump start the economy and push us into a New Aznazian Golden Age.
