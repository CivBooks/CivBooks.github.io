Welcome to the Lazuli Tower, a building of art, literature, and biology. If you are reading this, you've been granted membership to LazuliTowerAccess and can now interact with the building! There are rules to follow however. Please turn the page.
Page 3 of 25
Rules of Lazuli Tower: 1. Do not leave the tower with anything that you did not come into the tower with. Do not steal books or blocks or paintings. The exception to this rule is if you purchase something on Floor 10.
Page 4 of 25
2. Do not alter the building unless given permission by SamBonusG. That's it, those are the rules. Keep reading if you would like a quick summarization of each individual floor.

Floor 6: Petting Zoo This floor is where all the friendly animals live together in harmony. There are dual balconies and a water fountain, allowing for the animals to get plenty of space to be happy in. They are all named after the fourteen holy helpers from the
Page 10 of 25
Roman Catholic religion. With the exception of Shawn the sheep and Reynard the fox, both animals that randomly spawned in the building one day without the owner's knowledge. There is a chest on the floor with free sugar you can use to feed the animals.

Floor 7: The Library This is the floor in which you can access all the literature and music that the Lazuli Tower Foundation has recovered over the course of the server's existence. There is also a jukebox and old records on this floor, so you can listen to

music while reading your favorite Civ stories. If you have copies of a book that you notice we don't have, there is a donation chest in which you can place whatever literature you'd like to give to the tower.
