Ore Generation:§0 §0This server uses RandomOres. That means it won't show ores until you hit a block around it. This prevents x-ray and that means you won't be able to use it.
Page 3 of 7
Where to mine:§0 §0You can mine underground. I suggest mining above the bunker (large obby pyramid underground). There is a dirt block you can break and use to bring yourself up to the main large mining area. You can mine there or branch out.
Page 4 of 7
Diamonds:§0 §0Diamonds don't spawn like they normally do. And you also should know they can give you 2 diamonds for the price of 1. What to do when you find a diamond is DON'T BREAK IT WITH A NORMAL PICK! You must use a silk touch in order to get the most out
Page 5 of 7
of it. You can exchange 1 diamond ore for 2 diamonds in the National Ore Exchange. Diamonds also spawn in veins. You may get a bad vein which will give you not many diamonds. Or you could get an extremely lucky vein and get even 30 diamond ore.
Page 6 of 7
Getting tools:§0 §0You can get diamond picks for 2 diamonds or 32 iron ingots. I suggest stacking up on these as you'll run out pretty quick. You can also exchange iron ore in the Ore Exchange.
