This a testament of a pig-lamb, the grand sacrifice of the pink sheep, the death of a child of a god. There was once a Tree, THE TREE, a spruce tree to be exact. That tree was a holy tree and that tree had a just as so pious brother. That brother was Roinkelle.

Roinkelle was a benevolent diety and would be a sheep, an incredible red sheep. Roinkelle, though pious, was too quite adulterous & would bed a mortal sheep, a normal sheep, a white sheep, she was named Ba'athricia. The sheep Ba'athricia would, though allegedly normal was devious.
