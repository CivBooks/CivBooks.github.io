After years of studying the night sky from the observatory floor of Lazuli Tower, I think I am finally prepared to present my theory of why we are here. As I looked at the night sky of CivMC, I started noticing something rather peculiar. There were
Page 3 of 9
large areas of the night sky that seemed to be completely void. No stars, just darkness. I began to notice that these voids were taking on a hexagonal shape, and tessalating with other hexagonal voids. The stars were merely acting as the borders of these hexagons of darkness.
Page 4 of 9
It started to become more apparent that the night sky that we all see regularly may actually be made by design. I ran through so many scenarios in my head, but the only one that makes sense is that the sky, and possibly everything we know to exist, is merely a simulation.
