/----------------\ | §4THE CHIEFTANS§r | \----------------/ Eoahtl: Unknown timeframe in office, the creator of Yoahtl as a cultural entity. Held sway of a small band of followers and would travel from server to server.
Page 5 of 29
Wichita: The first chieftan within living memory. Eoahtl passed his position to Wichita, who kicked off the Shaman Age. This is when Quauhtli_Mia joined and the Yoahtlan meditation and tulpamancy began.
Page 6 of 29
Quauhtli_Mia: The last chieftan to be the sole political power in Yoahtl. He led Yoahtl during the Melonian era and is the first Chieftan to still play with us today. He was also the leader of the cultural community during the move to Civcraft.
Page 7 of 29
WackyAki: Quauhtli_Mia passed chieftainship in the beginning of CivClassics to WackyAki after he felt he was no longer the heart of Yoahtl. By this stage, the position was largely ceremonial and the real power was the Alcuahtl.
Page 8 of 29
/----------------\ | §4The Alcuahtls§r | \----------------/ Quauhtli_Mia: Created the position and reigned from the beginning of the post all through Civcraft 2.0 and into Civtemp. He was also the creator of the Excuahtl position detailed later.
Page 9 of 29
St_Leibowitz: Came to power in Civtemp and created the charter and Council. One of the more enduring figures and left a deep mark. Unfortunately he is no longer with us ingame and has moved on with life.
Page 10 of 29
Klasikrok: Headed Yoahtl after the Goodsprings-Yoahtl split in Devoted. Yoahtl did not last long in Devoted.
Page 11 of 29
St_Leibowitz (2nd time): Leib returned to power to head us on CivClassics. His inconsistent uptime and behavior led to the Day of the Coup, but his influence was still felt in that he reestablished the charter and established NYC after the Laconia Wars.
Page 12 of 29
bgbba (1st Time): Couped Leibowitz due to ongoing dysfunction and disaster in Axochitlan for about 10 hours, with Quauhtli_Mia signing off as Chieftan. This was overturned when Leibowitz logged in that night and bgbba ceded authority back to him.
Page 13 of 29
St_Leibowitz (3rd time): Leibowitz returned as Alcuahtl only to leave shortly after for vacation, leaving Asakuun in charge. This led to the Laconia Wars and Asakuun's eventual end as part of Yoahtl's community.
Page 14 of 29
Asakuun (Acting): Their leadership in lieu of Leibowitz led to Yoahtl's forced migration in the wake of the Laconia Wars. Realizing that Asakuun would not take a hard stance against the Laconians, bgbba replaced her as Deputy when Leib returned.
Page 15 of 29
Leibowitz (4th time): The final time Leibowitz was in charge of Yoahtl marked Yoahtl's rebirth largely under the direction of bgbba and other channers in the now-thriving NYC. He saw Yoahtl through the Vzis war and stepped down for the final time.
Page 16 of 29
TheOrangeWizard: Orange was a councillor who took up Leibowitz's spot as Alcuahtl since bgbba was out of commission at the time. He began the Undercity and Undermarket, and set in stone Yoahtl's growth policy. He also oversaw the Vallera Crisis.
Page 17 of 29
bgbba (2nd time): At least rising to the position, bgbba announced his Alcuahtlship would be relatively brief. He oversaw the 2nd Laconia War and the ongoing expansion of Yoahtl to become one of the greater powers of CivClassics.
