In the past fortnight, the Republic of Mt. Augusta has been attacked, incinerated, and nearly leveled. In these times of turmoil the residents of our great city have not given up. We have reassembled out of the ashes. In this spirit, we hope to dismantle

the old constitution, which has done nothing for the people of Mt. Augusta, and in its place we will ratify a constitution of our own making based on the tenets of democracy and equality.

I. Voting a. Voting Eligibility Anyone wishing to vote in the Augustan legal and political processes shall provide proof of residence or having a serviceable shop or other business within the state of Mount Augusta for at least 4 days prior to
Page 8 of 36
voting, and shall post such proof on the official Mount Augusta subreddit prior to or during any vote they wish to participate in.

b. Conduct of popular vote Voting shall take place on the official Mount Augusta subreddit. Each proposal shall be a clearly stated post on the subreddit, and shall be open to voting for no fewer than 48 hours. Each voter shall present
Page 10 of 36
proof of eligibility and either a "yes" or "no" vote as a comment on the porposal post. calls for changes to proposals may be presented as comments on the proposal post. Proposals on which voting is being conducted may not be edited, but may be
Page 11 of 36
resubmitted with or without changes if the vote fails. c. Publicization of Popular Vote All voting requirements and processes shall be noted in a clearly visible and accesible part of the city and on the official reddit.

II. Formation and Jurisdiction of Agustan Law a. Borders of the State of Mount Augusta The borders of the state of Mount Augusta shall extend 200m outside the current walls unless changed by process
Page 13 of 36
of law. All laws of Mount Augusta shall apply within its borders. b. Creation of New Laws New laws may be proposed by any eligible voters of the state of Mount Augusta. Such proposals shall be
Page 14 of 36
subject to a popular vote to determine passage. A majority of at least 3/4 of all votes cast shall be needed for passage. c. Fairness of Law No laws may be created that targets a group or individual or sets of differing standards of
Page 15 of 36
behaviour or punishment for a group or individual. No law may be applied to actions occuring before its passage. Any law violating this section shall be invalid.

d. Repeal or Amendment of Law by Popular Vote Movements to repeal or amend any law may be proposed by any eligible voter of the state of Mount Augusta. Such proposals shall be subject to a popular vote to determine passage. A majority
Page 17 of 36
of at least 3/4 of all votes cast shall be needed for passage.

III. Enforcement of Augustan Law a. Conduct of Trial Any individual accused of breaking the law of Mount Augusta shall be entitled to an open and fair trial by the voting body of Mount Augusta. A majority of at least 4/5 of all
Page 19 of 36
votes cast shall be needed for conviction. b. Defendant's Rights The defendant in a trial shall know the time of and be able to attend their own trial and present evidence in their favor. If there is not sufficient evidence to prove
Page 20 of 36
guilt, the defendant shall be assumed innocent and there shall be no punishment or conviction. No individual may be tried more than once for the same instance of the same crime unless they call for a retrial
Page 21 of 36
c. Limitations on Arrest and Detention In the case that an individual is reasonably suspected of theft, murder, or other malicious behavior, they may by imprisoned by anyone within Mount Augusta for no more than 48 hours prior to trial.
Page 22 of 36
If a trial is not held within 48 hours of imprisonment, the suspect shall be freed until the trial is held. d. Limitations on Punishment The punishment for breaking Augustan law shall be consistent and proportional
Page 23 of 36
to the crime committed. Maximum punishments defined in a law shall not be exceeded. e. Crimes Outside of Mount Augusta If an individual in Mount Augusta has broken the law in another state and has not had a trial, and such law has an
Page 24 of 36
equivalent in Mount Augusta, the individual shall be extradited or tried under Augustan law depending on the wishes of the state in which the crime was committed.

f. Retrial If there is new evidence that suggests that an individual convicted of a crime is innocent, they may demand a retrial in which any new evidence may be presented. Such a trial shall be conducted in the same way as a regular
Page 26 of 36
trial, and shall require the same fraction of votes for conviction. If the individual is not convicted in the new trial, they shall be cleared of the crime in question and all punishments shall be lifted immediately.

g. Pardons Pardons for any individual convicted of a crime within Mount Augusta may be granted by popular vote, with a majority of at least 3/4 of all votes cast needed for the pardon. Any individual so pardoned shall not be tried for the crime in

V. Government Offices All government offices shall be created and filled by popular vote. No office may have the power to contradict a popular vote for any reason.

VII. The Official Flag of Mount Augusta The flag of Mount Augusta shall consist of a blue rectangle with a red square in the top lift corner, or if on a flagpole, on the top corner on the side touching the pole. The red square shall be of such a size

that its side extends halfway across the smaller dimension of the flag. Acceptable materials shall include: blue and red wool, lapis blocks, and brick blocks.

VIII. Ratification and Amendments to this Document This document shall be ratified by a popular vote with at least 3/4 majority. Amendments to this document shall be treated in the same way as amendments to laws created under this document.
