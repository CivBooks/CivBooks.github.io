It is a well-known tale, the tale of the Pumpkin Jacks uprising. Almost no one knows the full story though, the driving force behind the Jacks. The organization that created the idea of the Jacks.

The organization known as the Nine. In this book I, NateMagic will tell you what actually happened in Lazuli. I will reveal the Nine and some of their members.

I will show you some of their true intentions, and leave others for you to realise. This book is a look into one of the most secret societies of CivCraft.

To start off we should go from the beginning, the founding of the Nine to the end of Lazuli. The Nine was founded by a group of like-minded power hungry individuals who all shared the same goal.
Page 8 of 47
This goal was the complete and total control of all events within CivCraft. From staged wars, to total slaughters of innocence. They silence the voices of those who speak out and realise what is happening.

They use their administrative powers to ban those whom oppose them. The group was formed while CivCraft had just began. They would infiltrate towns and push their members into positions of power with in these CivCraft societies.
