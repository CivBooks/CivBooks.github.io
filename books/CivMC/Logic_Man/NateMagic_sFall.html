Architect. Statesman. Traitor.§ NateMagic would fulfill many roles through his life during the First Era of CivCraft. The goal of this book is to outline the life
Page 7 of 40
of a notable character of the Civcraft story, and also give some insight into the history of key events that Nate was involved in. During the First Era, Nate would go from a wanderer, to a notable citizen of Lazuli, a war veteran,
Page 8 of 40
to a Lazulian council member, an architect of the first maester Citadel, to a traitor of all of Civcraft. So, we'll start at the beginning. ◊◊◊

§1§l Chapter II§r Journey to Lazuli
Page 10 of 40
NateMagic joined Civcraft around late April, early May of 2012. His journey began like most Civcraftians. He awoke in an unfamiliar place. Nate wandered for a few days before making contact with a
Page 11 of 40
friend of his, blizzxx, who was currently living in Lazuli, a small city at the time, which was rapidly growing. A perfect place for a new citizen of Civcraft to make a name for himself. Once he arrived in Lazuli, he, blizzxx,
Page 12 of 40
Squoitle, and Alexzandria began construction on their pirate ship, nestled on the coast of Lazuli's Newtown district. Nate arrived at an interesting time in Lazuli's history. War was afoot as a Lazulian splinter group known as
Page 13 of 40
"The Pumpkin Jacks" had recently launched terror attacks on the city. Nate was not in Lazuli long before he would make a name for himself in the blossoming Republic. ◊◊◊

§1§l Chapter III§r The Lazuli-Jack War
Page 15 of 40
On the 29th of April, 2012, at around 7 pm Eastern, the Pumpkin Jacks launched their terror attack on the city of Lazuli. The complete history of the war is a subject for another book, but this is where Nate would distinguish himself as a notable
Page 16 of 40
Lazulian. On the 5th of May, 2012, the Pumpkin Jacks along with a group of hired mercenaries known as Second Cell attacked Lazuli, pearling many of the citizens including the head of state at the time, SomethingSaucy,
Page 17 of 40
and most of the current Lazulian Council. They were able to successfully hold the city and repel counter-attacks from Lazuli's allies for several hours. A few days prior to the final attack,
Page 18 of 40
Nate assisted in establishing the first Lazulian snitch network around the city. This would help tremendously when the Jacks would launch their attack. Nate would be involved in defending his pirate ship against the Jacks, along with
Page 19 of 40
Lazulian council member Ttocs_is_Awe. Eventually, they were both pearled. The war was over, Lazuli was defeated. Nate almost left the city at that point, but no doubt he was now attached to his first real home in Civcraft.
Page 20 of 40
Out of the fires of war, Nate was now an established and respected Lazulian. ◊◊◊

§1§l Chapter IV§r Councilman NateMagic
Page 22 of 40
After the war, Founder* SomethingSaucy stepped down, and Ttocs_is_Awe became the new leader of the Republic of Lazuli, bringing in a new council with him. §o* Founder was the term used for the Lazulian head of state at the time.

On the 12th of May, 2012, NateMagic, along with lem0nland91111 and Logic_Man were sworn in as the new council of Lazuli. Nate would remain a councilman up until Lazuli's decline in late July of 2012.
Page 24 of 40
During this time (after the war, and before the exodus of Lazulian citizens) the Republic of Lazuli reached the peak of its power and influence. Nate would establish a mining colony near the world border to keep the government's
Page 25 of 40
coffers filled. After the fall of Lazuli, Nate would retreat to the old mining outpost to begin work on the Citadel. ◊◊◊

§1§l Chapter V§r The Citadel
Page 27 of 40
NateMagic began work on the Citadel sometime in late 2012, before becoming too busy with real life, when he relinquished head architectural duties of the Citadel to SomethingSaucy. He and Ttocs_is_Awe would be inspired by the Citadel to form
Page 28 of 40
form the Maester Alliance, which is still around to this day. This makes NateMagic a technical founder of the Alliance but the true extent of that is open for debate. Later on, Nate would come to disagree with what Maesters Saucy
Page 29 of 40
and Ttocs had done to the Citadel, even threatening to burn the flags housed in Maester Ttocs' Hall of Nations. Nate had become disillusioned with what Civcraft had become and his distaste for the Anarcho- Capitalists was now

§1§l Chapter VI§r The HCF Invasion of Civcraft
Page 32 of 40
The HCF (the common name given to several groups of players from a seperate server, Hard Core Factions) invaded Civcraft to disrupt the status quo and kill/pearl as many people as they could. NateMagic sold them weapons and armor
Page 33 of 40
and gave them access to his snitch network. It's up for debate whether or not they actually purchased them or if Nate simply gave them to HCF. Nate maintained his innocence as a simple seller of goods with no alternate
Page 34 of 40
intentions. However, I can tell you that Nate wanted the destruction of the Ancaps and wanted to force Ttk2 (praise be unto Him) to end the First Era and begin the Second Era. He felt the server had stagnated under
Page 35 of 40
the rule of the AnCap 'World Police' and thought that assisting an invasion force from a hardcore pvp server would bring the change he wanted so desperately. NateMagic would be pearled by Foofed on the 11th of December, 2012 (and again on
Page 36 of 40
the 21st of January). He maintained his innocence as a simple arms dealer using the free market, but very few people came to his side. His reputation never really recovered, even after the HCF fiasco died down.

Even so, I still call him friend.
