§1§lPart I: §1Of Bricks and Stone
Page 5 of 33
I stumbled upon this small but lively building early on in my travels. The citizens here greeted me cheerfully and offered me a small plot of land upon which to live. I graciously accepted the offer and
Page 6 of 33
proceeded to build my manor. A simple house, but not without small comforts. Made primarily out of cobblestone and wood gathered from the surrounding forest, it offers me a safe haven from the terror and violence of the outer world.
Page 7 of 33
I have a nice view of the eastern sea, and the mornings are nothing short of spectacular. I have become accustomed to this town, newly named Lazuli after the precious material found in a mine underneath.
Page 8 of 33
I shall live here as long as I am allowed and will protect it with my life, if need be. I also have some plans for a public building... I will have to see where my visions lead me.

§1§lPart III:§1 Of Death and Dogs
Page 15 of 33
I had one of the most life changing experiences today. I was mining in a system of tunnels and misstepped into a pool of lava. I felt my body burning and charring as I struggled to escape, but despite removing
Page 16 of 33
myself from the pit of lava, my body was beyond repair. I lay on the stone ground, allowing the darkness to take me, positive I had seen the last of my days. But as soon as I felt my body slipping away, I felt my mind and soul
Page 17 of 33
flying through the air, and before I knew it, I was waking up in my bed, as if it had only been a bad dream. Unfortunately, I knew for a fact that it had been real, for my armor and other normal possessions were gone.
Page 18 of 33
And I could feel the faintest trace of heat rising from my skin. Alas, I had died, but by the grace of some god I was allowed to return to this world. :: :: However, I quickly fell into a deep depression.
Page 19 of 33
I had always myself to be... invincible, unbeatable. To have my body be so easily destroyed was a grounding experience. I took to wandering the forests to the south, searching for zombies, spiders, and
Page 20 of 33
other monsters to kill. I was a wreck of my former self. However, during one of my walks, I stumbled upon a strange sight: a lone wolf. I had never seen such a beautiful creature. I offered it a bone from one of the
Page 21 of 33
skeletons I had killed, and it began following me around. I have decided to name him Tiberious. He shall be my companion for as long as he wishes. He definitely lifted me from my wretched state.

§1§l Part IV:§1 Of Contracts and Warfare
Page 23 of 33
A few days ago, a man arrived in our town. He was a wealthy man, and being impressed with Lazuli, made an inquiry about buying an apartment. Unfortunately, being a small town still, we had no such buildings.
Page 24 of 33
Instead, I offered my services as an architect and builder. He took one look at the Sapphire Library and accepted my offer. Giving me a few specific outlines, I began contruction on this large seaside house.
Page 25 of 33
However, in the midst of my work, we got word from the Rift that a known criminal had entered the area through the Nether. We quickly retrieved our weapons and armor and prepared for a fight.
Page 26 of 33
SomethingSaucy and Lenicis make the trek to Rift and saw the criminal off. After reporting his location to a team hunting him, we allowed ourselves to relax and breathe. Trouble was not gone, however.
Page 27 of 33
Not much longer, a pair of bandits entered the town and, as I slept, killed Lenicis and three others. One of the bandits was killed, but the other got away in the ensuing chaos.

These same bandits returned a few days later with a third member, but we were ready this time. We received a tip from the Rift and by the time the trio arrived, we lay in ambush, ready to strike.
