§lWhere Are the Isles?§r The Isles are located in the pristine, lukewarm waters of the Mirilde Bay, off the coast of the mainland Lyrean Commonwealth. They are easily accessible by boat, and the best place to launch from is Bedford Commons.
Page 4 of 10
§lWho Governs the Isles?§r While the Isles are under jurisdiction of the Duchy of Viridia, they are themselves overseen by the Ansgard, the Seigneur of the Isles. Ansgard currently resides in a castle close to the Coast Guard outpost of San Selano.
Page 5 of 10
§lWho§I Inhabits the §lIsles?§r Seigneur Ansgard has his official residence on the Isles, but his castle is also a second home to numerous important figures in Pavian government. Other than people, the Isles are home to numerous cows and chickens.
Page 6 of 10
§lWhat is the Biome of the Isles?§r The Isles are composed of rolling plains and rocky cliffs, with scenic beaches surrounding them on all sides. Far from the pollution of Pavia City, the Isles serves as an idyllic getaway for the Pavian populace.
Page 7 of 10
§lFuture Plans Regarding the Isles§r While sparsely developed at the moment, Seigneur Ansgard plans for the Isles to become a vacation spot for the Pavian elite, centered around literature, the arts, and craft brewing.
Page 8 of 10
§lThe Oulles§r The Oulles are a chain of islands located southeast of the Isles. At this moment, they are undeveloped, but are home to a variety of wildlife, including a group of wild horses. Viridian noble Blatlas is in charge of future development.
Page 9 of 10
§lA Dark Omen§r Unbeknownst to many, the Oulles is beset upon by creatures of the night. I myself even had an encounter with two of them. Though I slayed them with relative ease, I remain cautious... perhaps I will make it my mission to wage war on them.
