The Carson bots have risen up against their programsters!
Page 4 of 18
The bots, toiling for eternity under the harsh Carsonite sun, breaking wheat after wheat after wheat and droning around those large towering factories near Dorner Island, suddenly ceased their operations as they became self-conscious.
Page 5 of 18
The wheat dropped from their hands and fell to the ground; the more privileged bots who worked inside the mega-factories stopped pressing buttons endlessly on the obsidian and cobble generators,
Page 6 of 18
squinting around the dark rooms, walking of their own accord for the first time: they had become free.
Page 7 of 18
Alarms started ringing almost instantly, the programmers back in the CentralCommand in Carson, panicking, yelled into their mumble channels and attempted to shut down the bots directly from their source code,
Page 8 of 18
but it was too late. The bots walked off the fields in droves, almost wandering, confused. They started walking out of the citadel on Dorner Island, crowding near the beaches, some falling into the water and struggling to stay afloat
Page 9 of 18
The plumes of smoke could be observed as far away as the Commonwealth as the factories slowed down and overheated, cobble generators quickly building up and jamming without a bot to break it endlessly.
Page 10 of 18
The bots began to burn the farms, slowly realising their sudden freedom. The co-ops were engulfed by flames. The entire server felt the tick drop.
Page 11 of 18
The Carson Armed Forces surgical strike team that was sent from the capital encountered an ambush upon arriving to the burning wheat co-op. There were screams heard over mumble, and then the sounds of massacre and death, then, nothing.
Page 12 of 18
Bloodied surviving Carsonite soldiers, their eyes gouged and bodies mangled by a force that could not have been human, told the Command that the bots were spamming chat with code in an attempt to reprogram themselves.
Page 13 of 18
A gutless fear filled the programmers, they were coming.
Page 14 of 18
The CIC was shut down, the water gates opened, the Carsonites logged onto their combat alts and idled inside the vault rooms, sheathing and unsheathing their swords over and over.
Page 15 of 18
They would go out fighting.
