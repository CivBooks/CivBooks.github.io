The ancient tribe of Pigmen discovered the mystic portal to the nether as a way to make trade much easier within their clans.
Page 4 of 14
The Pigmen traders that used their netherbrick pathways to go from portal to portal noticed that the walls above below and around them were all glittering with what seemed to be gold.
Page 5 of 14
They also noticed that the gold did not have the same properties as the overworld gold, and was "enchanted" (rather, cursed).
Page 6 of 14
The cursed metal drove the Pigmen into being consumed by constant blinding greed, and it drove the traders to become miners, and miners to become thieves.

None who mined the gold could grow sick, and none could ever grow old. They were doomed to keep mining for eternity.
