Peter the Pinecone§0 §0 §0~A Fairy Tale~§0 §0 §0There once was a pinecone named Peter, who lived in a small clearing in a big forest. He was the most important pinecone in the whole, wide forest. Some of the other pinecones thought that they were more important than

Peter, but Peter knew better. Peter was the most important pinecone in the forest, and he knew it because while the other pinecones toiled away, he hung up high on the tree and watched them work. One day, Peter climbed down the tree and asked one of the

pinecones that was working at the bottom of the tree who the most important pinecone was. "Peter, of course it is me!" said the outcast pinecone. Peter did not like this answer, and he stamped the outcast pinecone into the ground to become an Evergreen.

The other pinecones saw this, and they did not like seeing Peter do such a thing. So Peter was told that he had to leave. And so Peter went, knowing he should prove the other pinecones wrong and let them know that without him, the forest would be sad.
