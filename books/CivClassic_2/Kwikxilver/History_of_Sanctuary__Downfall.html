§l Introduction§0§r §0§r §0 In Volume I of Sanctuary's history, our founder GameNBurger speaks of how Sanctuary stands for all, as a safe haven, a safe place. When he wrote those words we certainly were a safe place. However,

as I put these words to the page, Sanctuary is not safe. We have been griefed, ransacked and our citizens have mostly tried up. In my mind, this is the downfall of Sanctuary in CivTemp. Perhaps in 3.0 we will find a more prosperous existence.
