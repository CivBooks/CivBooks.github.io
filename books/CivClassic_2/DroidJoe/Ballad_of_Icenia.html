The story of Icenia's rise and fall is among the most tragic stories in all of Civcraft; yet not many know of it. In the beginning, there was darkness. A sole island under a fracturing empire with a unstable ruler at the crown.
Page 4 of 30
Icenia started as a colony of The Kaiserinreich. It was meant to be a fallout base for protection against the Romans. The leader, while paranoid, gave many opportunities for Icenia's citizens. Among those citizens was DroidJoe.
Page 5 of 30
DroidJoe had just moved into Icenia from the failed nation of Ultra Hardcore. With many of his associates gone from the world, DroidJoe moved to Icenia because of a business opportunity brought to him§0 §0by the infamous ChrisChrispie.
Page 6 of 30
ChrisChrispie was known as a fool to the world of Civcraft. When he had failed in countless places before, Kaiserin and its leadership brought him in when no one would. Icenia was a bustling colony and it had begun to receive attention.
Page 7 of 30
It became a new hub in the minus-minus for newfriends, a void left empty after Orion had become more and more inactive. While Kaiserin had helped Icenia tremendously, it had also restricted it.
Page 8 of 30
So much so that the citizens and the provisional government thought enough was enough, and declared independence§0 §0on July 8th.§0 §0 §0As a new nation, many flocked to see the newest nation of Civcraft.
Page 9 of 30
It had become a member of one of the largest alliances on the server and was, overall, well liked. However, this is a tragedy, not comedy or a romance. As more and more came to Icenia, the more and more unstable it became.
Page 10 of 30
Political groups came to be for the sole purpose it seemed to be, to cause instability. Along with that, ChrisChrispie had become more radicalized. Chrispie began to insult the former Ruler of the former empire it was under.
Page 11 of 30
Chrispie began to raid the now abandoned area of the capital of the Kaiserinreich. Icenia became too loud. It had begun to stick its nose way too far in other people's business.
Page 12 of 30
Along with this, the SPQR, long time allies with the villainous Nox, had begun to influence the citizens of Icenia into also supporting Nox. Most had agreed to be neutral, some supported Nox and one person decided to support World Police.
Page 13 of 30
That person, of course, was DroidJoe. After a few successful missions with WP to defeat Nox, DroidJoe was declared a risk to Icenia. With this, Droid was pearled because of it.
Page 14 of 30
The one true person trying to keep Icenia afloat, had been sent to the end indefinitely. When Droid returned to Icenia, it had been wrecked. The streets were covered in obsidian, the houses raided and no one had logged on in there in weeks.
Page 15 of 30
Icenia had failed.
