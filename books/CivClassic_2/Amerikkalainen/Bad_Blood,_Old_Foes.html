Yoahtl and Laconia share a fairly long history, much of which is marred by a poor relationship. The second Yoahtl-Laconia conflict has its roots in the first, so an understanding of the second requires some background knowledge.
Page 6 of 43
Laconia was originally founded by Yoahtlan expats and quickly grew to become an independent state in prior iterations of the civ genre. In this particular iteration, Yoahtl founded the city of Axochitlan in the outer Plus-Plus,
Page 7 of 43
far removed from the petty squabbles and raiders that characterized early Civclassics civilization. Laconia and Yoahtl shared shaky relations as neighbors in the early days of Civclassics. Accusations of
Page 8 of 43
alt-raiding arose soon after Axochitlan was raided in early July 2017, and the tension developed into outright conflict.§0 §0 §0After a short war, Laconia forced Yoahtl's hand. Despite a signed treaty
Page 9 of 43
guaranteeing Yoahtlan rights to Axochitlan and the surrounding Yoahtlan communities, the Yoahtlan government and the majority of the people made the decision to abandon their homeland in the Plus-Plus in
Page 10 of 43
search of new land. They eventually settled in what is today known as New Yoahtl City. Laonia extensively developed the infrastructure of their own capital Mt. Doom, as well as other hardened military sites in the Plus-Plus and
Page 11 of 43
the city of Danville, originlly Yoahtlan but ceded to Laconia in the treaty.

For a while, Laconia-Yoahtl relations were practically non-existent. Geographically, the thousands of blocks that now separated them enforced a peace stronger than any treaty.

In October, the outbreak of the Somber War forced communities worldwide to reconsider their position in global politics. Yoahtl and Laconia took two different paths in this regard -
Page 15 of 43
Yoahtl strove to maintain neutrality (at least in theory), while Laconia chose to ally with Lexington, a decision which greatly influenced the outcome of the second conflict.§0 §0 §0In Yoahtl, the soreness of the previous

Page 17 of 43

Starting in October and November, a subset of newer Yoahtlans with militaristic aspirations decided to convert the reconquest of the Plus-Plus from a topic rambled about by desperate oldfriends into a serious matter. In early December, it began.
Page 23 of 43
After a short time building basic military infrastructure, Amerikkalainen conducted a secret alt-raid on Mt. Doom without the knowledge of the Yoahtlan government. He managed to breach the majority of Laconian defenses because
Page 24 of 43
no Laconian happened to be online at the time. After privately conferring with Alcuahtl bgbba, the decision was made to claim this raid as the first acts in a new war with Laconia, and reinforcements were promptly sent. Before anyone else could
Page 25 of 43
arrive, however, Laconian TofeeDodger logged in. Amerikkalainen logged out quickly to avoid pearling, and the reinforcements were ordered to fall back to Yoahtl.
