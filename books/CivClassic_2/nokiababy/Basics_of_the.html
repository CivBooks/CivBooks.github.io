The basics of civclassic server 1. The world is only one. There are no nether and end worlds. 2. The world is devided into states. 3. Every time you die you will respawn in different place except if you don't set your spawn on a
Page 4 of 10
bed. 4. The plants grow on different biomes. For example carrot won't grow on planes. 5. Plants grow for hours. Clicking with left button with your seed in hand you will receive a msg in the chat that shows you how many hours will be needed.
Page 5 of 10
6. There is x-ray prevention in the server. Ores will remain invisible until you dont mine next to them. 7. If you find a diamond, there is a big possibility to find more around this one. 8. You can reinforce chest or placed block with the command /ctf, but first you should
Page 6 of 10
make a group. And second you should hold stone, iron or diamond in your hand while typing /ctf. After that every block you place will be reinforced. To break reinforced block you need to break it 50 times for stone, 150 for iron and 250 for diamond. To make a group type /nl.
Page 7 of 10
9. Factories are way to make more items with less resources. You can make a factory with placing chest, crafting table and a furnace next to each other. Then with a stick click on the crafting table and pick the factory you want to make. Most of them are expensive. More info - type /fm.
Page 8 of 10
10. If you misbehave in the chat or rob someone, there is a big possibility to be inprisoned. 11. For more information about civclassic, visit the wiki page.

The Kingdom of Kaltsburg is a monarchy in the Northwest quadrant of the server founded in 2018. It borders Varkonia, Lusitania, New Thoria and Lumiere throughout its territories. Founded in 2018 the nation has never participated in any conflict.
Page 10 of 10
Charactarized by its strict baroque and human-sized architecture, every town has a unique style to planning and design. The Kingdom provides free factories, materials, food and tools to its citizens. The flag is a horizontal 2-color with black on top and gold on the bottom.
